1. **Main energy consumption monitoring terminal equipment (DAQ-GP industrial grade energy consumption aggregation terminal for data acquisition and IoT)**  
As the core aggregation node for energy consumption data of all categories in the factory area, it integrates Protocol conversion, local large capacity storage, breakpoint continuation, national security transmission, and dual machine hot standby adaptation module; Built in 32GB industrial grade storage, local cache storage period ≥ 6 months of historical energy consumption data; Unified integration of power Gateway, OPC collection terminal, and handheld water collection terminal, and unified reporting of standardized energy consumption messages to provincial platforms.
2. **Supporting Collection Gateway (Data Acquisition IoT DAQ-GP Power Gateway, Data Acquisition IoT DAQ-GP Protocol Terminal)**  
Distributed deployment in the distribution room and LNG production workshop, responsible for collecting data from the power and natural gas systems Real-time, supporting offline caching, and automatically batch uploading historical data after network recovery.
3. **Auxiliary data acquisition terminal (DAQ-GP water handheld data acquisition terminal for data acquisition IoT)**  
Used for monthly data entry of water usage points without automated collection conditions, data is locally encrypted and stored, and can be wired synchronized to the main device.
**3. Collection and transmission operation standards**
1. Collection frequency: Electricity and natural gas data are collected every 15 minutes; Monthly summary and reporting of water usage data; Thermal data is collected once an hour; Coal is collected based on transportation batches and production shifts.
2. Discontinuous transmission mechanism: All DAQ-GP series terminals of data acquisition IoT devices are uniformly equipped with a disconnection cache transmission function. After network recovery, missing data is automatically transmitted in chronological order to ensure complete data timing without missing data.
3. Measurement accuracy control: The equipment has a built-in automatic alarm function for data deviation. When the power measurement deviation is greater than ± 2% or the natural gas measurement deviation is greater than ± 5%, the terminal will actively push abnormal reminders, and on-site debugging or measurement equipment calibration will be completed within 2 working days.
4. Unified clock synchronization: The main terminal of the data acquisition IoT DAQ-GP device automatically connects to the provincial NTP time server on a daily basis. All data collection Gateway and terminal synchronization in the entire factory area are synchronized with a unified timestamp, eliminating data confusion caused by multiple device time misalignment.
