In industrial fields such as steel, chemical, spraying, and waste disposal, various high-temperature exhaust gases are inevitably generated during the production process, including carbon dioxide (CO ₂), sulfur dioxide (SO ₂), nitrogen oxides (NOx), water vapor (H ₂ O), and incompletely burned carbon monoxide (CO) and other pollutants. If this type of exhaust gas is directly discharged without effective purification, it will not only pollute the atmospheric environment and damage the surrounding production ecology, but also directly threaten the personal safety of on-site personnel, becoming the core pain point of compliant production for enterprises.

With the increasingly refined and intelligent environmental supervision, relevant national management departments have clearly required key polluting enterprises to implement their main responsibilities for waste gas treatment, establish a sound waste gas treatment and monitoring system, achieve monitoring and data traceability of waste gas emissions, and actively accept law enforcement supervision. Relying on the core technologies of IoT (IoT) and Data Acquisition (DAQ), Shanghai DAQ-IoT networked exhaust gas monitoring solution, through the deep integration of front-end multi type Sensor and instruments, has built a "perception collection transmission analysis control" full chain digital intelligent supervision system, helping enterprises achieve green and compliant production, and promoting the deep integration of industrial digitization and environmental governance.

1. Full sensing and standardized data collection: The system can seamlessly integrate various front-end devices such as gas Sensor, temperature and humidity Sensor, flow meters, pressure gauges, etc. Real-time to collect waste gas[VOCs](https://zhida.zhihu.com/search?content_id=271370811&content_type=Article&match_order=1&q=VOCs&zhida_source=entity)Core pollution data such as concentration and emissions are synchronously captured, and relevant environmental parameters such as flue temperature, humidity, flow rate, and air pressure are synchronously captured. Through the built-in data acquisition engine, data standardization processing is completed, breaking data silos and laying the foundation for rapid integration of multiple platforms, achieving comprehensive perception and accurate collection of industrial waste gas data.

5.
