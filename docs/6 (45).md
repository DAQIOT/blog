3、 System core renovation and construction content
3.1 Monitoring and upgrading of key safety protection areas
Targeting 20 key safety protection areas of the diesel heavy machinery, new types of detection and monitoring equipment have been added to comprehensively improve monitoring accuracy and warning capabilities. The specific renovation contents are as follows:
-Spray painting related parts (spray painting exhaust duct, spray painting process): newly added spray painting air pressure, air volume, and temperature detection equipment, Real-time monitoring key parameters during the spray painting process to prevent safety hazards caused by abnormal parameters;
-Test related parts (test bench, large-diameter engine test, test exhaust pipeline): newly added test flame detection and arc induction equipment, accurately identifying dangerous signals such as flames and arcs during the test process, and achieving rapid warning;
-Load station: Add flame detection and thermal imaging monitoring equipment to comprehensively monitor the operation status of the load station and promptly detect safety hazards such as fires and overheating;
-Other key areas (detachment box, main power distribution, exhaust gas treatment facilities, monitoring and alarm devices, etc.): synchronously improve the configuration of monitoring equipment to achieve Real-time perception of the operating status of each area.
3.2 Integration of original monitoring and detection signals
To achieve resource sharing and reduce redundant construction, the original monitoring and detection signals in the park will be uniformly intercepted and integrated, and incorporated into the intelligent monitoring system. The specific content is as follows:
-Bohai Foundry: Intercept the original detection signals from 12 areas including the smelting electric furnace, pouring and painting post-treatment system, and natural gas station, and include them in the monitoring platform of this project;
-Key hazardous areas: Intercept the original video monitoring and warning system signals from three areas including oil and chemical storage, natural gas station, and methanol station, and integrate them with the signals from newly added monitoring equipment to achieve comprehensive video monitoring and warning.
