2. **Shanghai DAQ-IoT Network technology monitoring object: lightning arrester**
* **The function of lightning arrester**Protect electrical equipment (such as transformers, switches, etc.) from damage caused by lightning overvoltage and operational overvoltage. Under normal circumstances, lightning arresters are insulated from the ground; When an overvoltage occurs, it quickly conducts, releasing a huge current into the ground to protect the equipment behind it.
* **key parameters**Under normal operating voltage, a lightning arrester will have an extremely small current passing through, which is called **Full current** or **Leakage current**。
1. **How does this table work**
This Shanghai DAQ-IoT network technology monitoring instrument is connected in series with the grounding circuit of the lightning arrester to measure this critical **Leakage current**。
* **dial reading**You can see that the units on the dial are **MA (milliampere)**The scale ranges from 0 to 3.0 mA. This means it measures small currents in milliampere level.
* **Normal state**During normal operation of the power system, the pointer will indicate a very small value, usually at **0.5 mA ~ 1.5 mA** Between (specific values depend on system voltage and lightning arrester model). This stable and small current is normal.
* **Model Interpretation**：JCQ4A3-**10/800**-0Y
+ **10**It may indicate that the rated voltage of the system is 10kV (or other relevant voltage levels, to be determined on site).
+ **800**Usually, it indicates that the rated discharge current of the lightning arrester is 800A.

1. **Why do we need Shanghai DAQ-IoT technology to monitor this current?**
The monitoring of leakage current byShanghai DAQ-IoTis an important basis for determining the health of lightning arresters. If there is an abnormal current, it usually means that there may be a problem with the lightning arrester:
1. **damp**If the internal seal of the lightning arrester fails and moisture enters, it will cause a decrease in its insulation performance and leakage current**Significant increase**If the pointer reading continues to rise or suddenly becomes very large, this is a very dangerous signal indicating that the lightning arrester may have failed and needs to be replaced immediately.
2. **Aging/deterioration**As the running time increases, the valve plate of the lightning arrester will gradually age, and its characteristics will change, which will also lead to changes in leakage current.
3. **internal fault**Internal cracks, short circuits, and other faults can also be reflected in the current.

Once an abnormal increase or drastic fluctuation in current is detected, a warning can be issued in a timely manner, and maintenance or replacement can be arranged**Prevent equipment damage and even power grid accidents caused by the failure of lightning arresters**Ensure the safe and stable operation of the power grid.

2. **Shanghai DAQ-IoT Network technology monitoring object: SF6 gas**
* **The use of SF6 gas**SF6 is a colorless, odorless, non-toxic, and non flammable inert gas that has**Extremely excellent insulation and arc extinguishing performance**Therefore, it is widely used in high-voltage and ultra high voltage power equipment, such as:
+ **SF6 circuit breaker**
+ **GIS (Gas Insulated Composite Apparatus)**
+ **High-voltage switchgear**
* **Risk of SF6 gas**：
1. **suffocation risk**SF6 gas is about 5 times heavier than air. Once a leak occurs, it will accumulate in low-lying areas such as cable trenches and equipment room bottoms,**Replace oxygen in the air, causing local space hypoxia**There is a risk of suffocation and death for personnel entering such environments.
2. **Toxicity of decomposition products**At high temperatures where electric arcs are generated during the opening and closing of power switches, SF6 gas will decompose, producing toxic decomposition products such as thionyl fluoride (SOF2) and thionyl fluoride (SO2F2), which pose a great threat to human health.
3. **greenhouse effect**SF6 is explicitly stated in the Kyoto Protocol**Powerful greenhouse gases**Its global warming potential is carbon dioxide**23900 times**Strictly monitoring and sealing it withShanghai DAQ-IoTtechnology is also a requirement for environmental protection.
4. **summary**  
   This "SF6 gas concentration online Shanghai DAQ-IoT network technology monitoring system" is a key equipment for ensuring the safety of indoor workers in power facilities.

1. **Check gas volume (core!)**：

* **[Key]**：**As long as gas is seen, no matter how much (even if it is only 50mL), it must be recorded and reported!** This is ironclad evidence of potential internal faults.
1. **Check the gas color (to assist in determining the type of fault)**：
* **Colorless and odorless**It could be air.
* **Yellow, flammable**Wood insulation materials (such as insulation cardboard) overheat.
* **Grey, black**Insulating oil carbonizes due to arc discharge.**This is a very dangerous signal!**
* **white**Gas generated by insulation paper or cardboard malfunction.
1. **Check the oil level and leakage**：
* Confirm whether the oil level of the relay itself is within the normal scale line.
* Check for oil leakage at the interfaces of relays, windows, and lead cables.
1. **Check the control box and signals**：
* Check whether the signal relays for "light gas" and "heavy gas" are activated and whether the signals can be correctly uploaded to the backend monitoring system.
