**1、 Project background and requirements**
Background: In the production of precision automotive pipe fittings, copper plating[Machining leakage test](https://zhida.zhihu.com/search?content_id=270618001&content_type=Article&match_order=1&q=%E6%9C%BA%E5%8A%A0%E5%B7%A5%E8%AF%95%E6%BC%8F&zhida_source=entity)The control of process parameters in the core process is a key factor in determining product quality, among which the temperature of the copper plating process, the pressure and pressure drop data of the machining leak test are directly related to the product yield rate. To solve the problems of low efficiency and large errors in manual recording of core process data, poor performance ofReal-time, and inability to automatically collect and count the number of good products under traditional production modes, the enterprise proposes targeted data monitoring requirements and plans to carry out industrialData Acquisitionand visualization construction in the copper tube manufacturing workshop.
Implementation location: Qingdao, Shandong,[Qingdao Shiya Precision Pipe Fitting Co., Ltd](https://zhida.zhihu.com/search?content_id=270618001&content_type=Article&match_order=1&q=%E9%9D%92%E5%B2%9B%E4%B8%96%E4%BA%9A%E7%B2%BE%E5%AF%86%E7%AE%A1%E4%BB%B6%E6%9C%89%E9%99%90%E5%85%AC%E5%8F%B8&zhida_source=entity)It is a wholly-owned enterprise of Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan under the SEAH Group in South Korea, specializing in automotive precision pipe fittings. It was established in August 2007 in Huangdao District, Qingdao, and its core products are automotive brake pipes and fuel high-pressure steel pipes. It is a supply chain enterprise for several well-known automotive companies.
![图片](https://dev-to-uploads.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/articles/i9qz8yls8bgotmsksjcn.jpg)
Requirement: This project requires data monitoring of the copper tube manufacturing workshop: collecting temperature data from 2 PLCs and 1 heating furnace. Mitsubishi PLC Association[Lead-acid battery](https://zhida.zhihu.com/search?content_id=270618001&content_type=Article&match_order=1&q=%E9%93%85%E9%85%B8%E7%94%B5%E6%B1%A0&zhida_source=entity)The temperature of copper plating process for iron pipes is tested by Omron PLC corresponding to the machining workshop for leakage testing. After collecting data, it is displayed[host computer](https://zhida.zhihu.com/search?content_id=270618001&content_type=Article&match_order=1&q=%E4%B8%8A%E4%BD%8D%E6%9C%BA&zhida_source=entity)On the large screen, automatic collection and recording of the quantity of good products produced are ultimately achieved.
![图片](https://dev-to-uploads.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/articles/tzrd54aygsbr8eb77aay.jpg)![图片](https://dev-to-uploads.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/articles/y6tz1vkff2q6fj1rxx9u.jpg)
Implementation difficulties: 1. The PLC Data Acquisition needs to determine its status based on multiple data points and display the results after meeting the standards. However, the production data status bit is only retained for a short period of time and needs to be completed within 5 seconds for Data Acquisition, status determination, and result presentation. 2. The imported Japanese temperature instrument does not have a matching point table. It is necessary to refer to the point table of similar domestic instruments and repeatedly test and error to obtain the correct point table in order to achieve Data Acquisition.
