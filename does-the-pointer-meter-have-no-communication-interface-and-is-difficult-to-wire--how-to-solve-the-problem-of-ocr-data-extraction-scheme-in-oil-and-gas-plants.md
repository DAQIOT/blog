## **3、 Implementation Plan and Key Points**
## **（1） Technical Implementation Process**
1. Install a buckle type OCR recognition terminal on the meter;
2. Take timed photos of the dial at the set frequency;
3. Wireless transmission to the cloud for OCR analysis;
4. The data Real-time is pushed to the group's intelligent management platform.
The entire process does not require wiring, production stoppage, or modification of existing instruments, with a short construction period and minimal on-site disturbance, fully meeting the requirements of explosion-proof and safety management for oil and gas sites.
