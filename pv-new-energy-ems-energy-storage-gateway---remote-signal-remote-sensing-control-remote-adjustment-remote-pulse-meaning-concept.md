1. Remote signaling
Remote signaling refers to the remote status signal, commonly referred to as YX. Remote signaling refers to the monitoring and transmission of equipment status through remote transmission of signals. In the energy storage industry, remote signaling can be used to monitor the operating status and alarm information of energy storage equipment. Used to obtain status information of power equipment, collect and transmit various protection and switch quantity information. Contains switch status, alarm information, etc. of the device. Remote signaling is used to monitor the status of power equipment, such as determining whether the equipment is operating normally, whether there is a malfunction, etc.
Commonly used for measuring signals such as switch position signals, internal fault signals, protection device action signals, communication equipment operation status signals, etc.
Single point remote signaling and dual point remote signaling: Single point refers to using one digit to identify a remote signaling quantity, such as switch position. Only one normally open auxiliary contact is collected, with a value of 1 indicating closed position and 0 indicating open position; And the dual point needs to collect the positions of two auxiliary contacts, normally open and normally closed. When the normally open point value is 1 and the normally closed point value is 0, that is, 10, it is considered that the switch is in the closed position; When the normally open point value is 0 and the normally closed point value is 1, i.e. 01, it is considered that the switch is in the split position; When both position values are 1 or 0, it is considered that the switch position cannot be determined.

In the energy storage industry, telemetry can be used to monitor parameters such as voltage, current, temperature of energy storage devices, as well as information on the charging and discharging status of energy storage systems. Used to obtain measurement data of power equipment, collect and transmit operating parameters, including various electrical quantities (measured values of current, voltage, power and other parameters on the line). Telemetry is used to monitor the operating status of power equipment, such as monitoring the numerical values of parameters such as current and voltage.

Remote control includes control commands such as switching devices on and off, adjusting parameters, etc. Remote control is used for remotely controlling the operation of power equipment, such as remote switching, adjusting equipment parameters, etc.
